About Me

My name is Mika Aguilar, and I've been tinkering with computers since my parents bought our first one around 1998. I still have a 'Programming for Dummies' book and some notebooks filled with notes from way back then that I used to teach myself how to write code when I was around 14. I've always been fascinated by the seemingly endless potential technology brings to our lives, as well as a bit of a freak for organizing things nicely. Both traits translate well into developing applications for the web, and the field really excites me.

With the internet, building cross-platform applications that can enrich people's lives is the coolest thing ever. I've come a long way since I was a kid, and still have a long way to go. Right now I can write in php, java, C++, C#, javascript, jQuery, mySQL, html, and css.

I'm currently enrolled in classes at GRCC and living in Grand Rapids, Michigan with one more semester until I have an Associates in Web Design/Development. After that I'm planning on moving to Austin, TX to be with family and get a kickstart on my career.
